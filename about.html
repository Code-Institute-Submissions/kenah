Our Journey

Kenah is an independent craft business, that also provides workshops and learning opportunities to help and inspire others to develop their passion for art and design.

Kenah was created by Jess and Roxanne, two art and design lecturers. Together we have several years of teaching experience in art, illustration, pattern cutting, fashion design, screen printing and digital design. We initially developed Kenah because we felt inspired to use our creative skills to develop our own sustainable, unique brand of handmade gifts and stationery.
Our mission to take Kenah further is to run creative workshops for the community to access so they can learn some unique skills to inspire them, help them to develop their own business or just have a therapeutic, artistic experience.
